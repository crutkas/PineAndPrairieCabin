# Things to Do in the Area

Suncadia and the surrounding Cle Elum area offer year-round activities for every interest. From outdoor adventures to local dining, there's something for everyone.

## Outdoor Activities

### Summer Activities
- **Golf:** Two championship golf courses (Prospector and Rope Rider)
- **Hiking:** Numerous trails in the Cascade Mountains
- **Mountain Biking:** Miles of scenic trails
- **Fishing:** Local rivers and lakes
- **Swimming:** Outdoor pools and swimming areas
- **Zip-lining:** Suncadia Adventure Park

### Winter Activities
- **Skiing/Snowboarding:** The Summit at Snoqualmie (45 minutes away)
- **Cross-Country Skiing:** Trails throughout Suncadia
- **Snowshoeing:** Guided and self-guided trails
- **Sledding:** Designated sledding areas
- **Ice Skating:** Seasonal outdoor rink

## Local Attractions

### Nearby Towns
- **Roslyn:** Historic mining town (5 minutes) - Featured in "Northern Exposure"
- **Cle Elum:** Main town with shopping and dining (10 minutes)
- **Leavenworth:** Bavarian village (1 hour) - Great for day trips

### Points of Interest
- Roslyn Museum
- Suncadia Village shops
- Local wineries
- Scenic drives through Cascade Mountains
