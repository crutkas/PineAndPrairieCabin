## Final Walkthrough Checklist

Before you lock up, take 5 minutes to walk through:

1. **Bedrooms:** Linens stripped, windows closed, lights off
2. **Bathrooms:** Towels left as instructed, toilets flushed, lights off
3. **Kitchen:** Dishwasher running, trash out, counters wiped, lights off
4. **Living Areas:** Windows closed, fireplace off, lights off, decorations intact
5. **Outdoor:** Spa covered, grill off, furniture tidy
6. **Entryway:** All doors locked, alarm set (if applicable)

## Lost & Found
